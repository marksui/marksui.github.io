About

I’m Mark Sui, a graduate student in Electrical Engineering – Medical Devices & Systems at the University of California, San Diego. I hold dual bachelor’s degrees in Computer Engineering and Mathematics/Applied Science, and I also studied abroad at the National University of Singapore.
